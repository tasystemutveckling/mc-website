---
title: Junction box
description: The protective components between the battery and the inverter.
---

The junction box sits between the battery and the inverter and houses the protective components needed to connect and disconnect the power safely:

- **Main contactor** — connects and breaks the power.
- **Fuse** — protects against overcurrent.
- **Pre-charge resistor** — limits the inrush current when the inverter is connected, so the capacitors charge in a controlled way.
