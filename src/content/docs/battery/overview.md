Energy is stored in **96 lithium iron phosphate cells** (LiFePO₄) in the **33140** form factor (33 mm diameter, 140 mm height).

## Configuration

The cells are wired **48s4p** — 48 cells in series and 4 in parallel — giving a **154 V nominal** pack voltage.

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Cell type | LiFePO₄, 33140 |
| Cell count | 96 (48s4p) |
| Nominal voltage | 154 V |
| Discharge per cell | 75 A |
| Max output (parallel) | 300 A |

Lithium iron phosphate was chosen for its good thermal stability and long cycle life, at the cost of slightly lower energy density than other lithium chemistries.
