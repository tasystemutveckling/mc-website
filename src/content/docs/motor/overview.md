The central component of the project is a **brushless permanent magnet synchronous motor (PMSM)** in an **8-pole, 48-slot** configuration.

## Construction

- **Rotor:** 108 mm wide, 100 mm in diameter, fitted with neodymium magnets.
- **Housing:** aluminium with cooling fins.
- **Magnetic core:** 29-gauge electrical steel laminations.

A PMSM was chosen for its high efficiency and power density — properties well suited to a vehicle where weight and range matter.
